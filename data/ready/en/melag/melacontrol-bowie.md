MELAcontrol Bowie & Dick Test

Order no. ME01078

Summary

Top Characteristics

MELAcontrol Bowie & Dick Test (Size: 11.4 cm x 11.4 cm) is a test system according to EN ISO 11140-1 (type 2\) for routine control of the steam penetration of your autoclave. The practical test package simulates a laundry package weighing several kilograms being sterilized in an autoclave with fractionated pre-vacuum. The successful color change of the indicator in the test package takes place at 134 °C after 3.5 min. 1 test package is included in the scope of delivery.

The handling of the MELAcontrol Bowie & Dick test is very simple: Place the test package at the desired position in the load. Run the sterilization program and then check the result. With sufficient steam penetration, the indicator turns from yellow to black.

The new MELAcontrol Helix – Reliable Routine Testing for Class B Autoclaves

The MELAcontrol Helix is a professional solution for reliable routine monitoring of Class B autoclaves. The Helix test was developed in accordance with the strict requirements of the European standard DIN EN ISO 11140 and offers a validated and compliant method for testing steam penetration in hollow instruments—a crucial factor for effective sterilization.

By simulating the most challenging load conditions within the sterilization process, the Helix delivers highly meaningful test results and ensures maximum process reliability. A clear color change from blue to pink after successful sterilization enables fast, unambiguous evaluation and supports reliable documentation.

MELAcontrol Type 5 Indicator

Order no. ME01077

Summary

Top Characteristics

Indicator according to EN ISO 11140-1 for checking the sterilisation conditions (steam, temperature and time).

The indicator can be added to packaged instruments (e.g. in MELAfol, MELAstore) or placed directly in the chamber.

If the sterilisation conditions are observed, the indicator turns over after 16.5 min at 121 °C or after 3,5 min at 134 °C. The ready-to-use indicator is for single use and is supplied in a practical box with 250 indicator strips.
